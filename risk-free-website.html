AI-Optimised by Default

Built so AI engines can recommend you.

Every website we build includes schema markup — the structured data that tells ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini exactly what your business does, where you are, and who your customers are. Without schema, AI engines guess. With it, they can recommend you directly.

We also structure the content on every page so it answers the questions your customers ask AI search tools. FAQ sections, clear service descriptions, and a fast load time (PageSpeed 90+) all contribute to AI citation probability.

Most website designers do not think about AI search at all. OptiScale builds it in by default because that is what we do for every client.
