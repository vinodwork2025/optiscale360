What We Build Into Every Website

Schema Markup

We add structured data to every page. This tells AI engines exactly who you are, what you do, where you are, and what customers say about you. Without schema, AI engines guess, and usually ignore you.

FAQ Architecture

We research the real questions your customers type into search and AI tools. Then we build FAQ sections into your pages that answer those questions directly. This is one of the most reliable ways to get your content cited by AI platforms.

Speed Under 2 Seconds

Page speed is a ranking factor for both Google and AI indexing. Every site we build scores above 90 on Google PageSpeed. We test on both mobile and desktop before launch.

Mobile-First Design

Over 70% of searches in India happen on mobile. Every site we build is designed for mobile screens first, then scaled up to desktop. Not the other way around.

Google Business Profile

We set up and optimise your Google Business Profile, a major signal for local AI search results. Most businesses have incomplete or outdated profiles that hurt their visibility.

Analytics and Tracking

Every site includes Google Analytics 4 and Search Console setup so you can see exactly where your traffic comes from and how visitors behave.
