Your customers are asking AI for recommendations right now. "Best CRM for a small clinic." "Which agency should I hire in Bangalore." ChatGPT and Perplexity answer with three or four names. The hard part is you have no idea if you are one of them.

Why this matters now

AI answers are eating clicks. Your normal tools cannot see this. Google Search Console shows clicks. Rank trackers show positions. Neither can read a ChatGPT answer and tell you if your name was in it. That blind spot is the whole reason this tool category exists.

So the AI answer is often the only impression you get. If you are not named in it, you do not exist for that buyer.

The free way to check first

Before you pay for anything, do this. It takes an afternoon and tells you if you even have a problem.

Open ChatGPT and Perplexity. Ask the questions your customers ask. "Best [your service] in [your city]." "Top [your category] for [type of customer]." Run each one a few times, because answers change. Write down whether you appear, who does appear, and which websites the AI cites.

That gives you a rough baseline fast. The limit is volume. Twenty questions asked five times is enough to spot obvious gaps. Not enough to measure real trends. For that you need automation.

Two free tools help here:

  • HubSpot AEO Grader. A free one-click check of how ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini describe your brand. It scores you on share of voice and sentiment. Good for a first read.
  • Ahrefs Brand Radar. Has a free tier that shows AI crawler activity and AI search referrals to your site.

Paid tracking tools, by budget

When you want daily tracking, competitor comparison, and a record over time, here are the real options. Prices are early 2026 and change often. Check the vendor page before you buy.

Under ₹5,000 a month (about $60)

This is the right starting point for most small businesses and solo consultants.

  • Otterly.ai. Starts around $29 a month with a free tier to try first. Tracks ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, and Claude. Shows your mention rate, which pages get cited, and how you compare to up to five competitors. A sensible first paid tool.
  • Keyword.com AI. Around $24.50 a month, covering six AI platforms. One of the cheapest real trackers available.
  • HubSpot AEO. About $50 a month after the free trial, tracking 25 prompts across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini. Worth it if you already use HubSpot.

All three sit under ₹5,000 and give genuine tracking. For an Indian business watching spend, start here.

₹5,000 to ₹16,000 a month ($60 to $200)

More platforms, more prompts, deeper reports.

  • LLMrefs. Around $79 a month and tracks the widest set of AI engines. Think of it as a rank tracker built for AI answers.
  • Semrush AI Toolkit. Brings AI visibility tracking into the Semrush dashboard. The easy pick if you already pay for Semrush, since your AI and classic SEO data sit in one place.

Enterprise (₹40,000 a month and up)

Built for large teams that need to tie AI mentions to revenue.

  • Profound. Connects AI mentions to conversion data, so you see which AI-referred visitors actually buy.
  • Scrunch AI and AthenaHQ. Broad platform coverage and deep analytics for large brands.

Most small businesses do not need these. They are listed here so you know what the top of the market looks like.

The one metric that actually matters

When you read these dashboards, ignore "rank." Being named second in a single ChatGPT answer means nothing, because the next person who asks gets a different answer.

Track your visibility rate instead. That is the percentage of your test prompts where your brand shows up at all. If you appear in 30% of relevant questions and your competitor appears in 65%, that gap is the real story. Watch that number move over time. That is how you know your work is paying off.

A tool tells you the problem. It does not fix it.

This is the trap. Every tool on this list shows you a dashboard. Almost none of them fix your visibility for you. You still have to do the work. Better content, structured data, AI crawler access, and mentions on trusted third-party sites.

Frequently asked questions

How can I track if ChatGPT mentions my business correctly?

Use a tool like Otterly.ai or HubSpot AEO that runs your prompts daily and records when and how you appear. Some tools also flag when AI states wrong facts about you, like outdated pricing. For a quick free check, ask ChatGPT your customers' questions yourself and note the answers.

Which GEO tracking tools are worth it for a business in India under ₹5,000 a month?

Otterly.ai (around ₹2,400 a month) and Keyword.com AI (around ₹2,000 a month) both fit that budget and give real tracking. Pair either with the free HubSpot AEO Grader for a fuller picture. That stack costs less than ₹5,000 and covers all the major AI platforms.

Are there free tools to track AI visibility?

Yes. The HubSpot AEO Grader and the free tier of Ahrefs Brand Radar both give you a starting read. Manual testing in ChatGPT and Perplexity costs nothing. Free tools are fine for a baseline, but ongoing daily tracking needs a paid plan.

Is AI visibility tracking the same as keyword tracking?

No. Keyword tracking tells you where you rank on Google. AI visibility tracking tells you whether ChatGPT and Perplexity name you when customers ask. Different surfaces, different tools. You need both in 2026.

What should I do once I know my visibility rate?

Fix the gaps. Make your content easy for AI to read, add structured data, let the AI crawlers in, and earn mentions on other trusted sites. Then re-check your visibility rate in a month to confirm it is rising.
