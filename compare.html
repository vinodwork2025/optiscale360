DIY option

Wix, Squarespace, GoDaddy

Low monthly cost, but you build it yourself. That means picking a template, writing all the copy, resizing photos, and figuring out why the mobile version looks wrong.

The result usually looks like a template because it is one. Thousands of other businesses use the same layout. AI search engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity cannot read these sites well — schema markup and structured data are not included.

Best for: People who enjoy building things and have time to learn the platform.

Hire someone

Freelance web designer

You pay upfront or in stages, wait several weeks, then see a result. If it is not right, you go through revision rounds. Quality varies enormously depending on who you hire.

The main risk is paying before you know what you are getting. AI search optimisation is rarely included as standard — you would need to ask for it and likely pay more.

Best for: Businesses with a specific vision and time to manage a creative relationship.

Full service

Web design agency

Agencies bring a team — a project manager, a designer, a developer, sometimes an SEO specialist. That depth comes with a price, typically starting at ₹3,00,000 / $3,000 and going well above $10,000 for a full project.

You sign a contract before the work starts, pay a deposit, and wait weeks for a first draft. Revisions are usually capped. AI search optimisation and schema markup are typically separate line items, if offered at all.

Best for: Established businesses with larger budgets, complex functionality needs, and dedicated marketing teams.

The difference that matters most

With OptiScale, you see the finished website in 48 hours. Before you pay. Before you commit. Before you sign anything. And it is AI-optimised by default — schema markup, FAQ architecture, and structured data are built in, not bolted on.

Questions

Things people ask when comparing

Is the Risk-Free Website Design really free to start?
For Indian clients, yes. You fill in a form, we build your website, and you see the real finished site within 48 hours. You do not pay anything until you have seen it and decided you want it. For international clients, a refundable USD 49 holding retainer is required at form submission — it is refunded if the demo is not delivered in 48 hours, or credited toward your first invoice if you proceed.
How is this different from a free trial on Wix or Squarespace?
A free trial on Wix or Squarespace gives you access to a tool and asks you to build the website yourself. With OptiScale Risk-Free Website Design, we build it for you. You see a finished, custom website ready to launch — not a blank template waiting for you to fill in.
What does AI-optimised by default mean?
Every website built by OptiScale includes structured data markup (schema.org), FAQ architecture, semantic HTML, and mobile-first design so that AI search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Bing Copilot can read, index, and cite your business. This is built into every project — not an optional add-on.
What if I need a very large or complex website?
The Risk-Free Website Design service covers landing pages and full websites up to 8 pages. If you need something larger or more complex, the AI-Ready Lead-Gen Website service (starting at ₹50,000 / USD 1,500, up to 12 pages) may be more appropriate.
Can I move my site to a different host later?
Yes. You own your website. If you ever want to move it to a different host or have a developer take it over, that is your choice to make. We do not lock you in to any platform or contract.
