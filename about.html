Why OptiScale Advisors Exists

AI search is changing how people find businesses. Hundreds of millions of people now ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI for recommendations instead of scrolling through ten blue links. When someone asks "who is the best CA firm in Bengaluru?" or "top web developer near me", the AI gives a direct answer — naming specific businesses.

Most Indian businesses are invisible on these platforms. Their websites lack the structured data AI engines need. Their content does not answer the questions people are asking. Their business information is inconsistent across the web. The result: their competitors get recommended instead.

OptiScale Advisors was founded in 2024 to solve this problem. We audit your AI search visibility across all five major platforms, fix the issues that are keeping you invisible, and build AI-ready websites that get you recommended.
