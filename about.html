Vinod's Story

With over 15 years of hands-on experience in operations optimization, Vinod has been the driving force behind OptiScale Advisors' mission to help startups scale without breaking. His journey began at Stanford, where he studied Industrial Engineering with a focus on process optimization.

After cutting his teeth at fast-growing startups like Airbnb and Stripe during their hyper-growth phases, Vinod witnessed firsthand the operational challenges that can make or break a scaling company. He saw brilliant founders struggle with inefficient processes, team coordination issues, and the overwhelming complexity of rapid growth.

This experience led him to develop the OptiScale methodology - a systematic approach to operations scaling that combines proven business principles with AI-powered automation. Today, he leads a team of specialists who share his passion for turning operational chaos into competitive advantage.

Education: Stanford University - Industrial Engineering
Certifications: Lean Six Sigma Black Belt, AI Strategy Certificate (MIT)
