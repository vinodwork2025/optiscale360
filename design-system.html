We deliver the full audit report within 5–7 business days. This includes a detailed breakdown of your citation presence across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Bing Copilot.
What businesses benefit most from GEO optimization?
Any local service business — clinics, law firms, consultants, interior designers — that gets leads from search. If a potential client asks an AI "find me a [service] in [city]", you need to appear in that answer.
Do you work with businesses outside India?
Yes. We work with clients in India, the US, UK, Canada, and Australia. All communication is async-first via email, with calls scheduled on Calendly.
