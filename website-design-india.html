How Much Does Website Design Cost in India?

A professional small business website in India costs ₹40,000 to ₹1,50,000. Cheap template sites run ₹15,000 to ₹50,000. Agency-built business sites start around ₹80,000 and go past ₹2,00,000. Ecommerce stores begin near ₹50,000 and climb fast with features. The huge range exists because two sites that look the same can be built completely differently underneath.

Ecommerce store ₹50,000 – ₹2,50,000+ Selling products online

A freelancer usually charges less for the same scope, often ₹15,000 to ₹80,000. An agency charges more but brings a team, a process, and someone accountable after launch.

Why Two Quotes for the Same Website Are ₹50,000 Apart

Because they're not the same website. They just look alike in a screenshot.

The cheap quote is usually a ready-made template with your text dropped in. Fast and fine for a basic presence. The higher quote includes custom design, proper mobile work, fast loading, SEO structure, clean code, and lead capture built in. From the outside both are "a website." Underneath, one is a business tool and the other is a digital poster.

Three things drive the price up: how much custom design you need, how many pages, and whether real content writing is included. That last one surprises people. Most quotes don't include writing your content, and good copywriting for a ten-page site can add ₹20,000 to ₹60,000.

The Hidden Costs Nobody Puts in the Quote

The build price is just the start. Plan for these or they'll surprise you.

  • Domain name: ₹100 to ₹2,500 a year. The .in or .com address your site lives at.
  • Hosting: ₹2,000 to ₹50,000 a year depending on traffic and quality. Don't cheap out here.
  • Content writing: Often separate, ₹20,000 to ₹60,000 for a full site. Most cheap quotes exclude it.
  • Premium themes and plugins: ₹5,000 to ₹20,000 a year for licensed software.
  • Annual maintenance: Updates, backups, security, and fixes, usually 15 to 20% of the build cost per year.

A good provider tells you all of this upfront. One who hides it and wins you with a low headline price will find ways to bill you later.

Why Most Websites Bring Zero Enquiries

You can spend a lakh and still get no leads. Here's why it happens.

The site loads slowly, especially on mobile, where most Indians browse. Visitors leave before it opens. There's no clear next step, no obvious phone number, no WhatsApp button, no simple form. The visitor doesn't know what to do, so they do nothing. The pages talk about the company instead of the customer's problem. And there's no way for Google or AI search to understand the site, so nobody finds it in the first place.

A website that brings customers does the opposite. Loads fast. Works on a phone. Says clearly what you do and who it's for. Makes contact one tap away. Frequently Asked Questions

How much does website design cost in India in 2026?

A professional small business website costs ₹40,000 to ₹1,50,000. Basic template sites run ₹15,000 to ₹50,000. Agency-built sites start around ₹80,000. Ecommerce stores begin near ₹50,000 and rise with features. The range is wide because two sites that look identical can be built very differently underneath, which changes both cost and results.

How much does a small business website cost in India?

Most small business websites cost between ₹40,000 and ₹1,20,000 for a professional, mobile-friendly site with 5 to 15 pages and basic SEO. A simple template version can be done for ₹15,000 to ₹50,000. Below ₹15,000 you're usually getting a template install with little customization and no real strategy.

How long does it take to build a website in India?

A basic 5-page site takes 2 to 3 weeks. A professional business site usually takes 4 to 8 weeks. Larger or ecommerce sites take 8 to 12 weeks or more. The biggest delay is almost always content. If your text, images, and brand details are ready before the build starts, everything moves much faster.

Should I hire a freelancer or an agency to build my website?

A good freelancer suits a small, clearly defined project and costs less, often ₹15,000 to ₹80,000. The risk is continuity. An agency costs more but brings a team, a process, and accountability after launch, which matters if your business depends on the site for leads. Match the choice to how important the website is to your revenue.

Why are some websites so cheap and others so expensive?

Cheap websites are usually ready-made templates with your content added. Expensive ones include custom design, mobile and speed optimization, SEO structure, clean code, and lead capture. They can look similar in a screenshot but perform completely differently. Price reflects what's built underneath.

Is WordPress or a custom-coded website better?

For most Indian small businesses, WordPress is the practical choice. It's flexible, affordable, and easy to update yourself. Custom-coded sites give full control and better performance for complex needs but cost much more to build and maintain. Unless you have specific advanced requirements, WordPress covers what most businesses need.

What are the hidden costs of a website in India?

Domain (₹100 to ₹2,500 a year), hosting (₹2,000 to ₹50,000 a year), content writing (often ₹20,000 to ₹60,000 if not included), premium themes and plugins (₹5,000 to ₹20,000 a year), and yearly maintenance. Always ask what the quote does not include, because that's where surprise bills come from.

How much does website maintenance cost per year in India?

Website maintenance typically costs 15 to 20% of the build price per year, or roughly ₹15,000 to ₹60,000. It covers security updates, backups, plugin upgrades, bug fixes, and hosting management. Skipping maintenance is the most common reason sites get hacked, break, or slow down.

Do I pay before or after the website is built?

The usual practice in India is 50% upfront. We do it differently: you see your finished website first, approve it, and only then pay. If you're not happy, you don't pay. This removes the biggest risk in commissioning a site, paying before knowing whether it's any good.

How many pages does my business website need?

Most small businesses need 5 to 8 pages: home, about, services, and contact. Adding individual service or location pages helps with SEO because each can target what specific customers search for. More pages isn't automatically better. What matters is that each page has a clear purpose and a clear next step.

Will my website show up on Google automatically?

No. A website doesn't rank on Google just because it exists. It needs proper SEO structure, fast loading, mobile-friendliness, and content built around what people search for. Ask whether basic SEO setup is included in your build, many cheap quotes leave it out entirely.

What makes a website AI-ready?

An AI-ready website can be read and recommended by tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI. It has clean schema markup explaining who you are and what you do, consistent business details, and content written as clear answers. Most websites built today aren't AI-ready, so building one now puts you ahead as more buyers search through AI tools.

Why is my current website not getting any enquiries?

Usually one of four reasons. It loads slowly on mobile and visitors leave. There's no easy way to contact you. The content talks about your company instead of the customer's problem. Or it isn't built for search, so people don't find it. Fixing conversion is often worth more than spending on more traffic.

Can I update the website myself after it's built?

Yes, if it's built on a system like WordPress. Ask for this upfront and ask for a short handover or training session. Sites built without an easy editor lock you into paying the developer for every small change, which adds up fast.

What do I need to give the designer to start?

Have these ready: your logo and brand colours, text or key points for each page, photos of your work or team, contact details and locations, and examples of websites you like. The more you prepare upfront, the faster and cheaper the build, because the designer isn't waiting or guessing.
