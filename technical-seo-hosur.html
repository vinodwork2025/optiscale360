Questions businesses ask about technical SEO

Why is my website not showing on Google at all?
Usually technical: pages blocked from indexing, duplicate site versions (www and non-www both live), broken canonicals, or a sitemap never submitted. The site looks fine in a browser the whole time, which is why owners never know. Our free check finds it in minutes.
What does the audit cost?
Technical SEO audit from ₹7,500: full crawl, Search Console analysis, speed and mobile review, schema check, top-10 fix list. Complete digital presence audit including local SEO and AI discoverability from ₹15,000.
What is schema markup and do I need it?
Code that tells Google and AI systems exactly what your business is, where it is and what it offers. It powers rich results and AI citations. Most Hosur websites have none, making it one of the highest-return fixes available.
Does speed really affect rankings?
Twice over. Google uses Core Web Vitals as a ranking factor, and slow sites lose visitors before loading. Most mobile users abandon pages over 3 seconds. We target under 2.
Do you fix the issues or just report them?
Both. Take the fix list to your own developer, or have us implement it. Implementation is quoted after the audit, so you know what each fix costs before deciding.
