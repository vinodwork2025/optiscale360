What actually makes AI engines recommend a business

It is not magic and it is not luck. AI engines extract and cite businesses that are structured for extraction.

1

Clear entity signals

Your name, location, services and numbers must be identical everywhere: website, Google profile, directories. AI engines triangulate. Inconsistency means you get skipped.

2

Answer-shaped content

AI engines lift answers from pages that ask and answer real questions with specifics: prices, timelines, numbers. Vague marketing copy is invisible to them.

3

Machine-readable structure

Schema markup tells AI exactly what you are, where you are and what you offer. It is the difference between being understood and being guessed at. Most Hosur websites have none.
